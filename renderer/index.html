About this app

This is a web-based application that allows the user to manage employees and assign them to students.

With this app, you can easily view and edit employee information, such as their contact details and availability. You can also assign employees to tutors and track their information.

This is designed to be user-friendly and easy to use, making it the perfect tool to manage tutor employees and tutoring sessions.
